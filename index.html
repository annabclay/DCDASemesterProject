The Performance of Vulnerability

How Emotional Vulnerability is Strategically Edited in Reality Television

Research Question:

How is emotional vulnerability strategically performed or edited in reality television to increase audience engagement and monetization?

Project Abstract

Hypothesis: Emotional vulnerability in reality television is strategically amplified through production and editing techniques, leading to increased audience engagement and higher levels of social media interaction and monetization.

The Central Insight

Reality television's greatest achievement is not capturing authentic moments—it's creating the illusion of authenticity. Through deliberate editing, pacing, and narrative framing, vulnerability becomes a product, engineered for maximum engagement and profit.
